## Slide 8 — 51 Trust Score

> **Strategy Maps**

# The 51 Trust Score. A score you can trust.

Scores key vendors across up to 95 data points for a quantified, defensible basis for vendor comparison. **No other platform offers this.**

**Use cases:**
- **Vendor comparison** — Compare companies side by side using a single consistent metric.
- **Due diligence shortlisting** — Use score tiers to quickly filter down to institutional-grade vendors (7.5+) worth deeper evaluation.
- **Risk assessment** — Drill into pillar-level scores to spot specific strengths or weaknesses.
- **Ongoing monitoring** — Track how scores change over time as companies evolve.

**Visual:** Circle vendor profile, overall **8.6/10** rating, gauge from Weak → Caution → Variable → Positive → Strong. Pillars shown:
- Product & Service (90%)
- Risk & Maturity (90%)
- Technology & Innovation (90%)
- Enterprise Readiness (90%)
- Tabs: All / Overview / Trust Score / Compliance & Risk / Announcements / Products / Ecosystem / Competitors / Financials.
